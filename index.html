About Us

Syracuse-Based.
Women-Led. Mission-Driven.

SarahCuse Solutions LLC was founded with a clear mission: to provide reliable, professional business support services while breaking barriers for women entrepreneurs in the federal contracting space.

Certified WOSB — Eligible for federal set-aside contracts under the SBA Women-Owned Small Business Federal Contract Program.
Community Rooted — Proudly based in Syracuse, NY, serving Onondaga County and the surrounding region.
Reliability First — Government agencies depend on us for accurate, on-time, compliant service delivery every time.
Growing with Purpose — We are committed to expanding our capabilities and workforce to serve more agencies and create local jobs.
